Minesweeper, the way it should feel on a phone. The board you know,
rebuilt with a clean type-forward interface, crisp light and dark themes,
and controls tuned for one thumb: tap to reveal, long-press to flag.

Pick **Beginner**, **Intermediate** or **Expert**, then beat the clock.
There are no ads, no sign-ups and no data collection — just the game.
